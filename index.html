About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Barishal is a growing hub of technology and innovation. Students here practice competitive programming regularly and develop strong problem-solving skills. Many of our graduates are now working in the software industry, some in government IT sectors, and even in world-class companies like Google. Others are contributing through remote jobs with international firms — making us proud everywhere.
